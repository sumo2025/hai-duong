About Us

Built on the water.
Driven by reliability.

Founded in 2014 and headquartered in Hai Phong, Vietnam, Hai Duong Maritime began with small coastal vessels and has steadily grown into an operator of ocean-going Tween-Decker bulkers across Asia Pacific trade lanes.

Our fleet is classed by Nippon Kaiji Kyokai (NK) and managed to ISM Code standards. Every voyage is backed by an experienced chartering desk and a hands-on operations team - ensuring schedule reliability and cargo integrity on every fixture.

Primary trade routes span China, Japan, South Korea, Southeast Asia, India, Bangladesh, East Africa, and the Persian Gulf, with principal cargo in steel products, project equipment, and general dry bulk.
