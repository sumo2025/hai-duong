About Us

Built on Experience.
Driven by Reliability.

Founded in 2014 and headquartered in Hai Phong, Vietnam, Hai Duong Maritime began with small vessels operating primarily in Southeast Asia. Over the past decade, the company has steadily grown into a reliable operator of ocean-going Tween-Decker bulkers serving major trade lanes across the Asia Pacific region.

Our fleet is classed by Nippon Kaiji Kyokai (NK) and operated in full compliance with the ISM Code. Every voyage is supported by an experienced chartering team and an in-house operations team, ensuring schedule reliability and cargo integrity on every fixture.

Our primary trade routes cover China, Japan, South Korea, Southeast Asia, India, Bangladesh, East Africa, and the Persian Gulf. Principal cargoes include steel products, project equipment, and general dry bulk.
